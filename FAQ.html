Where do the lessons take place?

The lessons are remote and take place on the Outschool website. Helen teaches the class online on a video web conference where the children can actively participate and learn. There are a multitude of activities and optional homeworks Helen sets for class attendees to do through the file sharing feature. All classes aim to be fun first but very helpful and educational for your child.

How much do the lessons cost?

The lessons usually cost around £5 for group sessions and around £20 for 1 on 1 sessions. We accept other currencies as forms of payment and try to make our online sessions accessible and have appropriate times for all time zones.

What lessons do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of lessons here at Learning with Love. I have a particular interest in Maths, English, History, Geography and the 11 plus exam however I am open to feedback for popular subjects that students/parents request for me to cover.

What makes you qualified to teach my child?

I graduated from Oxford with a Bachelor of Arts degree and then went on to achieve a Masters degree in Primary education. I have been teaching for 15 years at British schools but I am taking a break from my full time teaching job to look after my 2 young children.
